Lasagna

Here is my grandma's recipe for lasagna

Blending mozzarella and ricotta cheese with Italian tomato sauce
and cremini mushrooms and layers of al dente pasta sheets.

Ingredients:

  • 32 oz Tomato sauce
  • 16 oz Mozzarella cheese
  • 16 oz Ricotta cheese
  • 12 Cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 Package lasagne sheets, cooked al dente
  • 1 Bunch of fresh parsley
  • 6 Fresh basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Line lasagna pan with layer of lasagna sheets
  2. Spoon a generous amount of tomato sauce over noodle
  3. Spoon an even layer of ricotta cheese over sauce
  4. Layer more noodles
  5. More sauce
  6. Layer mushrooms
  7. Layer mozzarella cheese
  8. Repeat process until lasagna fills to the top of the pan
