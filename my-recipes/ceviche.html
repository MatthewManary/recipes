Ceviche

Tio Pepe's Famous Ceviche

Fresh white ocean fish sliced thin and tossed in lime juice
with with a spicy bite of fresh chilis and cilantro.

Ingredients:

  • Freshly caught ocean white-fish (we use red snapper)
  • Freshly squeezed lime juice (enough to cover)
  • Fresh green chilis like jalape√±o or serrano, diced
  • Half of a red onion, thinly sliced
  • Small bunch cilantro, roughly chopped
  • Salt
  • Olive oil (optional)

Steps:

  1. Slice fish into thin bite-sized pieces
  2. Season with salt
  3. Toss with onions
  4. Cover with lime juice
  5. Mix in cilantro and let marinate for 5 minutes
  6. Drizzle with a hint of olive oil (optional)
